Privacy Policy for Url Editor

Effective Date: 2025-01-02

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that is collected and recorded by Url Editor ("we," "our," or "us") and how we use it. By using our Chrome extension, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension requires the following permissions to function effectively:

  • ActiveTab: This permission allows us to access the current active tab in your Chrome browser. We utilize this permission solely to modify the URL query as per your requests.
  • Storage: This permission allows us to save user preferences and settings locally on your device. The data stored is used exclusively for enhancing your experience with the Url Editor extension.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To enable the functionality of the extension (editing URL queries).
  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To improve our extension based on user feedback and usage patterns.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once the data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
